FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to launch a campaign?

Creating a campaign on FundSpark is completely free. A small platform fee applies only to successfully funded projects.

When do I receive the funds?

Funds are transferred to your linked account within 5–7 business days after your campaign ends successfully.

Can backers get refunds?

Refund policies depend on the campaign. Many creators offer refunds if a campaign doesn't reach its goal.

Is this a real payment system?

This is a frontend demo. All donations and transactions are simulated in your browser for demonstration purposes.
